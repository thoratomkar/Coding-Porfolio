VIT Student Committees


Vidyalankar Institute of Technology is home to many Student Committees, each of which has been formed to facilitate an environment of improvement and development of all VITians. These committees range from various fields like sports, technical, literary, and cultural. In a fair, transparent process which is widely publicized throughout the college, selection of the core team of the Committees is done. In order to ensure that the Committees which are formed by the Institute for the welfare of the students are functioning well, a mentor oversees the working and progress of each committee.

In this section the activities of various Student Committees are described under four broad heads of Student Development Committees, Student Chapters, Student Council and Enrichment.
